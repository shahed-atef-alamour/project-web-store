Exchange and return

*What are non-refundable and exchangeable products?

According to the instructions of the Ministry of Commerce and in order to preserve public health for all:

The consumer is not entitled to exchange and return care and beauty products, accessories and perfumes. Gift-wrapped at your request.

But if you receive an order other than your order, or the product is of poor quality, different from the picture or description, or damaged during transportation and shipping

We bear all costs of compensation to the customer:

In the event that the order was made, but the customer made an error while ordering in choosing the color or size

In this case, there is no exchange, and the customer bears the shipping costs in the event that he requests the product or the appropriate size for it, and in appreciation of it, he will be given a discount coupon on the new product.

*How can I replace or return a damaged product?

We sincerely apologize for any product you received that was damaged during transportation and shipping, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order.

And send pictures of the broken (damaged) product The case will be followed up by our team immediately
